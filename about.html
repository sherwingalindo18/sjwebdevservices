SJ Webdev Services is an independent web studio that designs and hand-codes websites for small businesses — with the care, speed and attention a big agency can't match.

our story

Small studio. Senior work. No shortcuts.

SJ Webdev Services started with a simple observation: most small-business websites are either bloated template installs that load slowly and look like everyone else's, or expensive agency builds where the client never talks to the person doing the work.

We do it differently. Every site we ship is designed on purpose and written by hand — clean HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript, with no page builders, no plugin roulette and no framework weight. The result is a website that loads instantly, ranks well, and can't break from a bad update.

Our clients are mostly restaurants, pubs, salons and service businesses across the United States — owners who care about the details and want a site that finally looks like the business they actually run. Alongside client sites, we've also built custom internal systems: booking flows, check-in tools and small web applications shaped to the way a team really works.

When you work with us, you work directly with the builder. One point of contact from first call to launch — and we stay reachable after.
