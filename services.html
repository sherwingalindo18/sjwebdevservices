good to know

Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical website take?

Most landing pages take a few days, while a multi-page business website usually takes one to three weeks depending on scope and how quickly content and feedback come back.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on scope. A focused landing page sits at the lower end, a multi-page business site in the middle, and ecommerce or custom functionality at the top. Tell us your goal and budget and we'll recommend the best-value approach — every quote is fixed before work begins.

Do you work with my existing brand?

Absolutely. We build around your existing logo, colours and brand voice. If you don't have a brand yet, we can shape a simple, consistent visual identity as part of the project.

Can I update the website myself afterwards?

Yes. We hand over clean, well-structured files and can walk you through everyday content updates — or handle them for you under a maintenance plan.

Do you offer support after launch?

Always. Every project includes a support window after launch, and ongoing maintenance plans are available for updates, fixes and improvements over time.

Why hand-coded instead of WordPress or a site builder?

Hand-coded sites load faster, rank better, can't break from plugin updates and are far harder to hack — there's simply less to go wrong. You get a site that's exactly what your business needs and nothing it doesn't.
